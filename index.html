Drive Music Player
Play Your Music Files1 Stored in Google Drive

How To Use

In the text box below, find and enter the ID for the Google Drive folder you would like to use and click the "Authorize / Login" button. The ID can be found by taking that last part of the folder's URL, for example, drive.google.com/drive/folders/12345-abcde , has an ID of 12345-abcde .
