Drive Music Player - Play Music Files in Google Drive

Find and enter the ID for the Google Drive folder you would like to use and login below. As an example, drive.google.com/drive/folders/12345-abcde has an ID of "12345-abcde". Alternatively, "root" can be used to browse from your top-level folder.
