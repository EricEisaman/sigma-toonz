Drive Music Player
Play Your Music Files Stored in Google Drive

Stream music from your own Google Drive folder! Buying digital music from services like Bandcamp is a great way to support artists. This app allows you to browse and listen to music that you have stored in Google Drive. See a demo below!

How To Use

In the text box below, find and enter the ID for the Google Drive folder you would like to use and click the "Authorize Drive Access" button. The ID can be found by taking that last part of the folder's URL. For example, the folder, drive.google.com/drive/folders/12345-abcde , has an ID of 12345-abcde .

Authorize access to Drive
  1. The recommended folder structure is something like my-folder / artist / album / track 1 | track 2 ... , but other structures should work as well.
  2. For extra security, you will be required to authorize/login with Google each time you use the app. This is because the app lacks a dedicated backend and the access token is not being stored.
